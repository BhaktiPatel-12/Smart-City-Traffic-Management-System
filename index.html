Problem Statement

Metro City is facing severe traffic congestion, frequent accidents, delayed emergency services, and rising air pollution due to poor traffic management. The municipality needs a scalable, secure, and intelligent SCTMS to optimize signal timings, reduce delays, improve safety, and support future city planning.

Project Objectives

Detect congestion and accidents automatically using AI.
Adjust traffic signal timings dynamically based on density.
Provide alternate routes for commuters.
Prioritize emergency vehicles at signals.
Adapt traffic control based on weather conditions.
Store historical traffic data for prediction and planning.

Security and Privacy

  • Encrypt sensitive data in transit and at rest.
  • Use role-based access control for administrators and operators.
  • Protect citizen privacy by minimizing personal data storage.
  • Maintain audit logs for access, alerts, and system changes.
  • Deploy redundancy, backups, and failover for high availability.

System Maintenance

Future Scope

The system can later be extended with smart parking guidance, voice-based travel assistance, vehicle-to-infrastructure communication, pollution-based lane control, and predictive traffic simulation using historical data and AI.

Conclusion

The Smart City Traffic Management System provides a complete digital solution for congestion reduction, accident response, emergency prioritization, and data-driven planning. With AI, IoT, weather integration, and secure 24×7 monitoring, Metro City can build a safer and more efficient transport network.
